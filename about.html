    About Us

    Founded in 1966 by Stephen Hillenburg in Toronto, Canada, Coffee Express introduced Canada to its darker roasted Arabica coffee in blends including French Roast and grades appropriate for espresso drinks. Coffee Express offers freshly roasted beans, brewed coffee and espresso beverages, as well as bottled cold brew.
